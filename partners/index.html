Partners

AIM Enterprise partners with premier companies that encompass the whole spectrum of real-time communication applications and services. Our partners include providers of Unified Communications and Collaboration solutions, communication management gateways and archiving and compliance platforms, among others. These relationships help ensure that our customers receive support across the spectrum of communications applications that touch AIM. Customers using AIM Enterprise partner solutions and that have support agreements with AOL receive technical support to identify and resolve an issue regardless of where it lies in a customer's communications infrastructure.

AIM Enterprise is always interested in partnering with best of breed companies in the communication and collaboration space. If you are interested in partnering with AIM Enterprise, please contact us.

Actiance® is a global leader in communication, collaboration, and social media governance. Its platform is used by millions of professionals across dozens of industries. With the power of communication, collaboration, and social networks at their fingertips, Actiance helps professionals everywhere engage with customers and colleagues so they can unleash social business.

The Actiance platform gives organizations the ability to ensure compliance for all their communications channels. It provides real-time content monitoring, centralized policy management, contextual capture of content and smart archiving which improves the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of eDiscovery and helps protect users from accidental or malicious leakage of information.

Cisco® Jabber is a unified communications (UC) application that lets you be more productive, anytime, from anywhere on any device. You can quickly and easily find people, see if and how they are available, and collaborate using your preferred method, including instant messaging (IM), voice, video, voice messaging, desktop sharing, and conferencing. Cisco Jabber interoperates with AOL Instant Messenger enabling Jabber and AIM users to share online presence information and send instant messages.

FactSet helps the world's best investment professionals stay ahead of global market trends, access extensive company and industry intelligence, and monitor performance with desktop analytics, mobile applications, and comprehensive data feeds. Purpose built for the global financial professional, FactSet's Instant Messenger service goes beyond traditional messaging to let users share news, charts, and other FactSet financial data components. In addition, FactSet is a member of the exclusive AOL enterprise partnership program, which allows FactSet IM users to easily import and communicate with contacts in the AOL community. Learn more at www.factset.com, and follow us on Twitter: www.twitter.com/factset.

IBM Sametime® software is a social communications offering providing immediate access to expertise in your social network through integrated voice, video, meetings as well as federated instant messaging and presence with services such as AIM. Sametime yields increased efficiency and effectiveness in communication by improving the overall collaboration experience with customers, colleagues, and partners around the world.

Through Microsoft's federation relationship with AOL, organizations that use Microsoft Lync as their unified communications platform can enable their Lync users to connect with users of AIM. End users benefit from being able to connect with customers, partners, and vendors on their own terms. IT benefits from supporting a single real-time communications client while maintaining the control, compliance, and archiving features of Lync. For more information on Lync, see lync.microsoft.com.

Pivot™ powerful instant messaging software drives profitability for thousands of customers in hundreds of buy and sell-side trading rooms. Pivot's solutions are used to manage instant message workflow, extract and organize relevant market information and disseminate it efficiently, even if the information is embedded in casual instant message conversations. Improved workflow, transparency and information means better trading decisions.

Pivot is the only instant messaging software designed exclusively for market professionals. Pivot's customers include almost 8,000 brokers, traders, analysts and sales traders at more than 300 top buy and sell-side banks, hedge funds, asset managers and trading companies. Pivot focuses on high touch markets, largely cash equities, equity options as well as OTC energy and weather derivatives.
