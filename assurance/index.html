AIM Assurance

Assurance is knowing that when you call AOL for help, you will be working directly with an experienced team of engineers that is dedicated to supporting the needs of business AIM users. As an extension of your network, we understand that our network must be reliable and that if issues occur, they must be resolved quickly and efficiently. With specialized network tools and access, our engineers can check the real-time status of the various AIM systems, diagnose individual user issues down to the protocol level and help pinpoint their origin. We pride ourselves on being responsive to your needs and provide a range of support coverage options, all with guaranteed response times.

AIM Assurance offers three support programs tailored to the unique needs of your company:

  • IM Pro is for companies that use standard AIM clients or custom clients based on the standard AIM protocol (OSCAR). IM Pro includes a commercial access license for the AIM Network and standard technical services and support available M-F from 8:00 am to 8:00 pm ET.
  • UC Pro is for companies that connect to the AIM Network through supported Unified Communications and Collaboration (UC&C) solutions (e.g., Microsoft Lync, IBM Sametime, Cisco Jabber, etc.). UC Pro includes standard technical services and support available M-F from 8:00 am to 8:00 pm ET.
  • Omni Pro is for mixed environments that have both standard AIM clients\custom AIM-based clients and supported UC&C solutions. Omni Pro includes a commercial access license and expanded technical services and support available 24x7x365.
